Research

I am an ecosystem ecologist and biogeochemist with a focus on the terrestrial carbon cycle. I study how processes which regulate soil carbon storage respond to climate change across a range of ecosystems. I am currently a graduate student with Ben Taylor in the Harvard OEB department.

About

I am an ecosystem ecologist and biogeochemist with a focus on the terrestrial carbon cycle. I study processes which regulate soil carbon formation and loss in different ecosystems and across scales, from microbial to global. Originally from Southern California, I am currently a grad student with Ben Taylor in the Harvard OEB department.
